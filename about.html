Meet Mike

Honesty · Value · Integrity
Add a photo of Mike or the shop here

Born and Raised in Quakertown.
This Community is Personal.

I grew up here. My family lives here, shops here, goes to school here. When you bring your car to me, you're not dealing with a chain — you're dealing with a neighbor who has a real stake in making sure you leave happy.

Before opening Auto Repair by Mike in 2016, I served 8 years in the United States Air Force as an Aerospace Maintenance Journeyman. That's where I learned that doing the job right the first time isn't optional — it's the only way to work. That mentality came with me to the shop.

After the Air Force, I spent 10+ years working at BMW, Honda, KIA, and Toyota dealerships, as well as independent shops. I've seen what customers get charged at big dealerships. I know what things actually cost. And I'm not going to tell you that you need something you don't.

I've continuously invested in the latest computerized diagnostic equipment so I can work on modern vehicles just as confidently as anything older. Whether you drive a 2024 BMW or a 1998 Civic, I've got you covered.
