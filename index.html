About Mike

Built on Trust,
Backed by Experience

Born and raised in Quakertown, I'm proud to provide quality auto repair services to my community. My family and I work and live here — this isn't just a business, it's personal.

I've spent 10+ years as a mechanic across dealerships including BMW, Honda, KIA, and Toyota, as well as independent shops. Before that, I served 8 years in the United States Air Force as an Aerospace Maintenance Journeyman — which means attention to detail and doing the job right the first time are non-negotiable.

We've continuously invested in the most up-to-date computerized diagnostic equipment so we can handle modern vehicles as well as classics.
